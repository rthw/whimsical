color palette and graphics

The color palette is composed of contrasting colors to reflect the dualism of the studio's calm professionalism and rebellious boldness. Together, orange and green colors are reminiscent of autumn, the time when nature is quiet but bright.

Graphics repeat the font and logo aesthetics. It allows to set up the rhythm through the identity mediums, whatever elements they contain.
implementation in web
contrast in layout, typography, colors
The concept of the identity is fully reflected through the website:
  • High-contrast fonts in style and size: delicate grotesque + sharp serif.
  • Stories told via minimalist texts followed by glossy gradient blocks.
  • Graphic elements support type aesthetics and highlight bright nature of gradients embodying sharp ideas of the studio.
